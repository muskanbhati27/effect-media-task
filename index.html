About us

Our Story

Learning matters. It elevates people, society and nations. TopTalent LMS has always believed in building a bedrock of transformation by creating a learning ecosystem that is simple, supportive and adaptable to evolving needs. Built on 30 years of experience in learning structures and delivery, TopTalent is creating a new world of guided learning solutions that uplift people, businesses and government organisations. Based in Texas, USA, the TopTalent Group partners with some of the most reputed training and certification vendors and has recognition from the U.S. Department of Labor as well as being licensed for individual training and guidance by the Texas Workforce Commission. With our location agnostic solutions TopTalent is able to provide supported online solutions across America and e-Learning solutions across the world.

Learning maximized by the HumanConnect

Much like having a personal master brewer who listens to you and understands your business objectives, TopTalent L&D Optima assigns a constant human presence to customise, curate, collaborate and co-create the LMS solution that is best for your organisation. This technology and human blended solution makes it scalable, relevant and optimised for your audiences and learning ecosystem, in a way no other LMS program can master. Thats the power of HumanConnect at work.
